About Me

Hello! I'm Yusuf, and I'm on an exciting journey of career transition from medicine to IT.

I was born in Turkey in 1994. I started Cerrahpasa Medical School in 2013. After graduating in 2019, I embarked on a diverse medical career. I worked as a General Practitioner (GP), an Emergency Doctor, and a Public Health Doctor in Turkey, gaining valuable experience in patient care and public health initiatives.

In August 2022, I achieved GMC (General Medical Council) registration, opening up new opportunities in the medical field. Subsequently, in February 2023, I joined the Cardiology department at the University Hospitals of Leicester as a Trust Grade Doctor, where I continued to expand my medical expertise.

However, my passion for technology and IT led me to make a bold career change. In September 2023, I decided to pursue a career in the world of Information Technology. I'm now actively building my skills in IT and exploring exciting opportunities in this field.

My journey reflects my adaptability, commitment to growth, and enthusiasm for tackling new challenges. I look forward to applying the problem-solving skills and dedication I developed in medicine to my IT endeavors.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. Feel free to explore my projects and get in touch to discuss collaborations or opportunities.
